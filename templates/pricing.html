Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard settings.

What happens if I downgrade?

You'll immediately lose access to premium features. Your API keys will be disabled, but your data remains safe.

Do I need a credit card for Freemium?

No! Sign up and start using Freemium tier without any payment information.

What happens to unused monthly quota?

Unused quota does not roll over. Each billing cycle starts fresh with your plan's full quota amount.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes — we have an automatic refund guarantee. If you don't receive your SMS code, your credits are automatically refunded. For payment refunds, requests within 14 days are processed within 5–7 business days.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Paystack. Cryptocurrency payments (BTC, ETH, SOL, LTC) are also supported.

What is the auto-refund guarantee?

If your verification session times out without receiving an SMS code, we automatically refund the full cost to your wallet balance — no support ticket needed.
