Dispute Guidelines
Valid Reasons for Disputes:
  • Service Not Received: Verification failed or phone number not provided
  • Incorrect Charge: Charged wrong amount
  • Duplicate Charge: Charged multiple times for same service
  • Quality Issue: Service quality below expectations
Dispute Process:
  1. Submit dispute with detailed description
  2. Our team reviews within 24-48 hours
  3. You'll receive email notification of decision
  4. If approved, refund processed within 3-5 business days
Most disputes are resolved within 48 hours. Please provide as much detail as possible.
