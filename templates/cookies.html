Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

Last Updated: January 18, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our service.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration Required
Essential Authentication, security, session management Session / 30 days Yes
Functional Theme preference, language, settings 1 year No
Analytics Usage tracking, performance monitoring 2 years No

2.1 Essential Cookies

  • admin_token / token: Authentication (30 days)
  • session_id: Session management (session)
  • csrf_token: Security protection (session)

2.2 Functional Cookies

  • theme: Dark/light mode preference (1 year)
  • language: Language preference (1 year)

2.3 Analytics Cookies

  • _ga: Google Analytics user ID (2 years)
  • _gid: Google Analytics session ID (24 hours)

3. How to Manage Cookies

3.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

3.2 Opt-Out Links

  • Google Analytics: Opt-out

3.3 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from:

  • Logging into your account
  • Maintaining session state
  • Using secure features

4. Third-Party Cookies

4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site. Data is anonymized and used solely for improving our service.

4.2 Payment Providers

Paystack may set cookies during payment processing. Review their cookie policy at paystack.com/privacy

5. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of essential cookies. For non-essential cookies, we will request your consent through a cookie banner on your first visit.

Questions about our cookie policy? Contact us at privacy@namaskah.app
