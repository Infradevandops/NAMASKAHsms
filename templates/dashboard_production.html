FAQ

How long does verification take?

Most SMS verifications complete within 30-60 seconds. Voice verifications may take 1-2 minutes.

What if I don't receive the code?

You can retry with the same number, try voice verification, or get a new number. Refunds are automatic for failed verifications.

How much do verifications cost?

Costs vary by service and country, typically ranging from $0.40 to $1.50 per verification.
