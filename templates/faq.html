Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Namaskah SMS verification service

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

▼

Creating an account is simple and free. Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, enter your email address, create a strong password, and verify your email. You'll get instant access to our platform with a welcome bonus.

Is there a free trial?

▼

Yes! All new accounts receive a welcome bonus of $5 in free credits. This is enough to test our service with multiple verifications across different countries and services.

What payment methods do you accept?

▼

We accept all major payment methods including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency. All payments are processed securely through Paystack.

SMS Verification

How long does it take to receive an SMS?

▼

Most SMS codes are delivered within 5-30 seconds. Our average delivery time is under 10 seconds. If you don't receive a code within 2 minutes, you can request a refund through your dashboard.

Which countries are supported?

▼

We support 190+ countries worldwide. You can view the complete list of supported countries in your dashboard. We're constantly adding new countries based on customer demand.

Can I use the same number multiple times?

▼

Each verification number is single-use for security reasons. After receiving your SMS code, the number is released back to our pool. You can request a new number for each verification.

What if I don't receive the SMS?

▼

If you don't receive an SMS within 2 minutes, you can request a refund from your dashboard. We'll credit your account immediately. If this happens frequently, contact our support team for assistance.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?

▼

Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for all data. Our infrastructure is GDPR compliant, and we undergo regular security audits. All data is stored in secure data centers with redundancy.

Do you log SMS content?

▼

We log SMS content for a limited time (24 hours) for delivery verification purposes only. After that, all content is permanently deleted. We never share this data with third parties.

Is Namaskah GDPR compliant?

▼

Yes. We are fully GDPR compliant. You can export or delete your data at any time from your account settings. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

API & Integration

Do you have an API?

What's your API rate limit?

Standard accounts have a rate limit of 100 requests per minute. Enterprise customers can request higher limits. Check your API documentation for current limits.

Is there webhook support?

▼

Yes. We support webhooks for SMS delivery notifications. You can configure webhooks in your dashboard to receive real-time updates when SMS codes are delivered.
