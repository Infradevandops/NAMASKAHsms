## Business Model Critical Issues

### Issue 1: Freemium Underpriced
**Current**: $2.22/SMS (provider costs $1.50-$2.00)
**Margin**: Only 10-32% (industry standard: 50-70%)
**Fix**: Increase to $2.75/SMS
**Impact**: +$530/month per 1000 SMS

### Issue 2: No Minimum Purchase
**Problem**: Users buy 1 SMS at a time
**Fix**: Minimum $5 purchase (2 SMS)
**Impact**: +25% average transaction value

### Issue 3: PAYG Makes No Sense
**Problem**: PAYG costs MORE than Freemium ($2.50 vs $2.22)
**Fix**: Remove PAYG or make it $2.00/SMS with volume discounts
**Impact**: +15% Pro tier conversions

### Issue 4: Pro/Custom Gap Too Small
**Current**: Only $10/month difference
**Fix**: Pro $25/mo, Custom $75/mo
**Impact**: +$40/month per Custom user
